Strawberry Milk

Description

A quick and easy recipe for strawberry milk!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups strawberries, hulled
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • chilled milk

Steps

  1. Chop strawberries and place into a blender. Blend until smooth, adding splashes of water if necessary.
  2. Pour blended strawberries into a small saucepan. Add sugar.
  3. Cook medium-low heat until sugar has dissolved and mixture has thickened slightly.
  4. Remove from heat and wait for the mixture to cool before using/storing.
  5. Add about 2 tbsp of mixture into a cup and top with 1-2 cups of milk adjusting ratio to personal preference. Mix well and enjoy!

    6. The leftover strawberry mixture can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
